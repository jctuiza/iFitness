Workout Sheet

Arms

Our arms workout combines targeted exercises for the biceps, triceps, and forearms. Through a mix of weight training and calisthenics, our program ensures balanced development and improved strength. Whether you're aiming for muscle growth or toning, our routines cater to diverse fitness goals and levels.

Chest

Our chest workout focuses on sculpting and strengthening the pectoral muscles through various exercises. With a blend of weight training and calisthenics, we offer a comprehensive approach to chest development. Whether you prefer bench presses, push-ups, or dips, our routines target the chest from multiple angles, promoting muscle growth and definition.

Legs

Our legs workout targets key muscle groups like quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. Combining weight training with bodyweight exercises, our program provides a balanced approach to leg development. Whether you're doing squats, lunges, or calf raises, our routines challenge your legs in different ways to promote muscle growth and strength.

Shoulder

Our shoulder workout focuses on all three deltoid heads and surrounding muscles. With a mix of weight training and calisthenics, we offer diverse exercises like shoulder presses, lateral raises, and handstand push-ups. This comprehensive approach strengthens and sculpts the shoulders, improving stability and reducing injury risk.

Back

Our back workout targets major muscle groups including the lats, traps, and rhomboids. Combining weight training with calisthenics, our program includes exercises like pull-ups, rows, and back extensions. By focusing on both horizontal and vertical pulling movements, we ensure balanced back development.
